The ‘Intimate’ Round Dining Tables

Back

Round dining tables are loved for its small and compact design. It provides a more personal space for small gatherings compared to other designs, such as rectangular, or oval. Round tables are considered the one that fits perfectly for personal dinner, either with friends or family. A small family should choose round dining table for their dining room. The round dining room table won’t waste space as many as other designs, which makes it suitable even for the smallest dining room. Overall, round dining table only able to give seats for about 4 people; but this ‘disadvantage’ is round dining table’s advantage. It gives you special space to enjoy and have a more quality time with your relatives.

For people who want to have a large banquet, round dining table is off from the chart. Even a large round dining table won’t be a good solution; they only made the participant to feel very far away from each other. For that purpose, it’s more recommended to get other designs, rectangular or oval may be the one that suit the needs. But hey, there is a little ‘hack’ for the round dining table to makes it able to hold more people. It’s called table leaves. Round dining tables will be able to have more seats with the table leaves; it’s a good way to prepare for something like sudden visits.

Still, even with table leaves, round dining table is not a suitable place to have large gatherings; the leaves often disturb or even ruin the table design. It’ll be more effective and recommended to get another table if you plan to have occasional large gathering. Round dining tables are able to give you private and intimate space when you’re dining with your relatives, something that’s much needed today. Such quality time with your beloved should be supported with the right table.
